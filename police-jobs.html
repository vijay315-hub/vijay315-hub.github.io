Police Job Eligibility

Police jobs are available for 10th pass, 12th pass and graduate candidates depending on the post. Some positions may require physical fitness tests, running tests and medical examinations.

Police Recruitment Selection Process

The selection process generally includes written examination, physical efficiency test (PET), physical standard test (PST), document verification and medical test.
