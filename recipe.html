Traditional Bengali Jhalmuri Recipe

Spicy Kolkata Street Food

What is Jhalmuri?

Jhalmuri is one of the most popular street foods in West Bengal. This spicy puffed rice snack is famous in Kolkata for its crunchy texture and mustard oil flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 green chilli
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon mustard oil
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice

How To Make Jhalmuri

  1. Take puffed rice in a bowl.
  2. Add chopped onion and tomato.
  3. Mix green chilli and coriander leaves.
  4. Add mustard oil and spices.
  5. Squeeze fresh lemon juice.
  6. Mix quickly and serve immediately.

Why People Love Jhalmuri

Jhalmuri is loved because it is cheap, quick, tasty and healthier than many fried snacks. The spicy Bengali flavor makes it one of India’s most famous street foods.
