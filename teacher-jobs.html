Teacher Job Eligibility

Teacher jobs generally require qualifications such as D.El.Ed, B.Ed, graduation or post-graduation depending on the teaching post. Some positions may also require TET, CTET or state-level teaching eligibility certificates.

Teacher Recruitment Selection Process

The selection process may include written examination, teaching aptitude test, interview, document verification and classroom demonstration depending on the organization and post.
