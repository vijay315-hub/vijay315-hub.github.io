Benefits of Jhalmuri

Healthy and Famous Bengali Street Food

Why Jhalmuri is Popular

Jhalmuri is one of the most loved street foods in West Bengal. People enjoy it because it is spicy, crunchy, affordable and quick to prepare.

Top Benefits

  • Low oil compared to fried snacks
  • Quick and easy evening snack
  • Affordable for everyone
  • Rich in flavor and spices
  • Can include fresh vegetables
  • Popular across India

Healthy Ingredients

Jhalmuri contains puffed rice, onions, tomatoes, coriander and lemon, which provide light nutrition and fresh taste.

Street Food Culture

Jhalmuri is deeply connected with Kolkata street food culture. It is commonly sold near railway stations, beaches and busy markets.
