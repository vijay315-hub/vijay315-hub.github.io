About Jhalmuri Bengal

Traditional Bengali Street Food Website

Who We Are

Jhalmuri Bengal is a simple website dedicated to sharing information about famous Indian street foods, especially traditional Bengali Jhalmuri from Kolkata and West Bengal.

What We Share

We publish content related to:

  • Jhalmuri Recipes
  • Indian Street Food
  • Kolkata Food Culture
  • Traditional Bengali Snacks
  • Trending Food Topics

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote Indian street food culture online through easy-to-read and informative content for food lovers across India.

Disclaimer

This website is an informational food blog and is not connected with any government organization.
