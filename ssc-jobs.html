SSC Job Eligibility

SSC jobs are available for 10th pass, 12th pass, diploma and graduate candidates depending on the examination and post requirements. Some technical positions may require specific qualifications or experience.

SSC Recruitment Selection Process

The selection process generally includes computer-based examination, descriptive test, skill test, physical test for some posts, document verification and medical examination depending on the recruitment rules.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for SSC jobs?
